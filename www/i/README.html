CC License Icons

Here you'll find license icons. Please hotlink to these images, over HTTPS directly to licensebuttons.net.

You'll find icons in a variety of sizes and colors:

  • p or l — this is for a (p)ublic domain deed or a Creative Commons (l)icense.
  • Next you'll find a directory for the license or deed of your choice (ie. by-sa, or cc-zero)
  • Next you'll find a choice of four background colors -- transparent, #000000, #eeeeee or #ffffff
  • Next you'll find a bunch of different colors and shades of grey, split up over three directories with hex-values for Red, Green and Blue
  • Finally, you'll find your image in three different sizes.

For example: https://licensebuttons.net/i/l/by-sa/eeeeee/66/22/11/

Comments? Suggestions? Really want a hot-pink CC icon for your platform? A different size? We're listening! Email info@creativecommons.org or make a GitHub issue.
